Instructions how to play Guess Who

  • Get Started: Enter your name and choose your difficulty level.
  • Difficulty Levels: Level 1 gives you 30 seconds and 5 mistakes, while Level 2 offers 5 seconds and 2 mistakes.
  • The Mission: Identify the secret character by asking smart questions.
  • Use Clues Wisely: Use the "Hint" button for the best strategic question.
  • Stay Relevant: Irrelevant questions will cost you a mistake point!
  • Win the Game: Finish before time runs out to reach the leaderboard.
